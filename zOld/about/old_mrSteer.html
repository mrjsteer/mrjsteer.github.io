Who on Earth is Mr Steer?

Mr Steer is a successful maths, economics and business studies teacher across the globe. As of February 2023, Mr Steer has been employed as a maths teacher in five different schools. Some students call him Mr Steer, others prefer to call him Mr J because it sounds cooler while being respectful.
